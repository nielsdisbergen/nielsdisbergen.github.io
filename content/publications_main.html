Publications

  • Assessing Top-down and Bottom-up Contributions to Auditory Stream Segregation and Integration with Polyphonic Music

    This work, published in Frontiers in Neuroscience, introduces, validates, and makes publicly available a novel paradigm designed to study auditory scene analysis. Discussed experiments make use of custom composed polyphonic music pieces to investigate auditory stream segregation and integration processes in humans. Alongside this publication we made the stimuli and tasks available via the Zatorre lab's website.
